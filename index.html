How LAZOJU works

LAZOJU connects movement with app access. The more you move, the more intentional screen time you can unlock. This helps reduce passive scrolling and encourages healthy routines.

Built for real life

LAZOJU is designed for general wellbeing, focus, self-discipline, families, and users who want more control over distracting apps without losing flexibility.
