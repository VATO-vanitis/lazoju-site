How LAZOJU works

LAZOJU connects movement with app access. The more you move, the more intentional screen time you can unlock. This helps reduce passive scrolling and encourages healthy routines.

Why LAZOJU needs Android permissions

LAZOJU uses a few Android permissions only to deliver its core experience of movement-based screen-time control. We aim to explain them as clearly and simply as possible.

Accessibility Service

This helps LAZOJU recognize when a user-selected restricted app is opened, so the blocker or warning flow can appear at the right moment.

Usage Access

This helps LAZOJU understand app usage timing needed for the wallet, unlock logic, and restricted-app handling.

Overlay Permission

This allows LAZOJU to display its blocker or warning screen above a restricted app when available time is used up or limits are reached.

These permissions are used to make LAZOJU work as intended and are not requested for unrelated hidden tracking.
